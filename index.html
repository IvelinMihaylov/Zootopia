OPENING HOURS

Zootopia is open every day all year round without a day off! Summer opening hours (from March to October) Ticket sale and entrance: 9:30–18:00 hrs All visitors must leave the Zoo at 19:00 hrs The Big cats enclosure, terrarium and aquarium, The Elephant House and The Monkey Houses are open from 9:30 to 18:30 hrs. The Administration office is working from 8:30 to 17:00 hrs Monday to Friday). The Zoo Veterinary Clinic is open from 8:30 am to 17:00 hrs (Monday to Friday). The Clinic has a separate entrance (from the side of str. Srebarna).

VISITORS’ RULES

Dear visitors, We kindly remind you that pets, bikes, scooters, rollerblades, segway, hoverboards, balls and balloons are not permitted in the Zoo. Please note that in Sofia Zoo is not allowed to: touch the exhibited animals, to feed them and to throw any objects to their enclosures. The animals in the Zoo are fed on carefully prepared and balanced diets. Feeding animals with sweets, bread and other treats can make them ill and even kill them! make photos and videos with professional cameras for commercial use without permission. visit the Zoo under the influence of alcohol or any other intoxicants. enter into restricted areas and/or areas protected by safety fence. Visitors under 12 years old must be accompanied by an adult. Please keep your entry ticket until you leave the Zoo and present your ticket on request. Thank you in advance for your understanding!
